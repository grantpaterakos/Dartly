Steel Tip Darts
⚖️ Weight: Typically 20–26g. Heavier darts are more forgiving for beginners.
🎯 Board: Sisal/bristle dartboard. Self-healing — darts leave no permanent holes.
🏆 Used in: All professional leagues (BDO, PDC). Standard for competitive play.
🔧 Tip: Measure from the face of the board, not the wall. Use tape or an oche marker on the floor.

Soft Tip Darts
⚖️ Weight: Max 21g. Lighter than steel tip — adjust your throw accordingly.
🎯 Board: Electronic dartboard with pre-moulded plastic holes. Auto-scores.
🏠 Great for: Home play, beginners, venues with kids — tips are safer if they miss the board.
💡 Tip: Replace worn plastic tips regularly — they break and cause bounce-outs.
Etiquette
🤫 Don't talk or move while someone is throwing
⏳ Wait until the thrower retrieves their darts before approaching
👀 Stand behind the oche when it's not your turn
🤝 Shake hands before and after every match
